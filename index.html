What is Tritanopia?

Tritanopia is a rare form of color blindness affecting approximately 0.01% of the population. People with tritanopia have difficulty distinguishing between blue and yellow colors. Blues may appear greenish, and yellows may appear pink or light purple. This condition significantly impacts the perception of web interfaces that rely on blue-yellow color schemes.
